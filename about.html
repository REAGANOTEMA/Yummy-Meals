CEO's Message

Hello! I'm Isaiah Mubazi, CEO of Yummy Meals Café, operating under HTTI SACCO. Our mission and vision guide every decision we make to provide exceptional dining experiences.

Our Mission

To serve authentic, delicious, and affordable meals while fostering a welcoming environment for students, locals, and the wider community. We aim to support local talent, create memorable dining experiences, and continuously innovate our menu to meet diverse tastes.

Our Vision

To become the leading student-oriented and community-friendly café in Jinja, recognized for quality, affordability, and authenticity. We aspire to be a hub where people connect, enjoy great food, and experience a warm and inclusive atmosphere.

Meet Our Head Chef

Our head chef blends traditional Ugandan spices with contemporary culinary techniques, ensuring every dish has both authenticity and innovation. Passion drives every plate.
